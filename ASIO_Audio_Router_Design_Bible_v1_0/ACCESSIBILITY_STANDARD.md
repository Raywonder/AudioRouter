# Accessibility Standard

Accessibility is infrastructure.

Rules:
- No required mouse actions.
- No visual-only controls.
- Every control must have a label.
- Every meter must have a text equivalent.
- Every route must be navigable from keyboard.
- NVDA, JAWS, and Narrator must be tested.
- Screen reader audio must be protected from accidental stream/record leakage.
- TTS must be independently routable.
- Emergency accessibility alerts must remain available in Safe Mode.
